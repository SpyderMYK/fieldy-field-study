## Addendum (same day): receiver deployed

Receiver ([`src/webhook_receiver/`](../src/webhook_receiver/)) deployed to an always-on Linux host as a systemd service, exposed via Tailscale Funnel on port 8443, archiving to ZFS. **[verified]** end-to-end from the public internet: authorized POST → 200 → JSONL line written; tokenless request → 403 (logged headers-only). Two operational observations for RQ1 analysis: the funnel proxy rewrites `remote_addr` to loopback — the true client IP arrives in `X-Forwarded-For` — and Tailscale adds a `Tailscale-Funnel-Request: ?1` header. The host's pre-existing tailnet-only HTTPS service on 443 remained tailnet-only after enabling the funnel on 8443 (checked explicitly).
