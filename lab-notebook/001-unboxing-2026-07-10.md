## RQ5 finding — on-box legal disclaimer [verified, photographed]

The box states: *"This device contains a microphone and it is the user's
responsibility to comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding
the recording of others."*

Fieldy explicitly assigns recording-law compliance to the wearer, on the
packaging itself. Notable in light of protocol v1.2's Colorado analysis: the
vendor provides the microphone and the cloud pipeline but disclaims the
consent problem entirely. Whether the app adds any consent tooling remains
open (Day 1 checklist §2).
